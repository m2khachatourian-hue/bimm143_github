  1. Which method gives your favorite results for the same data.dist dataset? Explain your reasoning.

The ward.D2 method gives the best results because it produces more clearly separated and compact clusters that better match the benign (B) and malignant (M) diagnoses. This method minimizes variance within clusters, leading to more meaningful and well-defined groupings compared to single, complete, or average linkage.
